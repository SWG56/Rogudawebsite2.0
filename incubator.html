Join the Incubator

Eligibility

  • Roguda graduate (within 2 years of completion)
  • Business concept related to fashion/textiles
  • Commitment to full-time entrepreneurship
  • Willingness to mentor future students

Application Requirements

  • Business plan or concept pitch deck
  • Portfolio of designs/products
  • Financial projections (1-2 years)
  • Letter of motivation
  • Completed POPIA consent form

Selection Process

  • Application review by advisory panel
  • Business pitch presentation
  • Feasibility assessment
  • Interview with incubator committee
  • Acceptance notification within 4 weeks
